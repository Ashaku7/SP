About SP Sports Academy

Welcome to SP Sports Academy, where we believe every child deserves the chance to discover their athletic potential! Our state-of-the-art basketball court and kids fitness center in Chennai provide a safe, fun, and nurturing environment for children to learn, play, and grow. With our expert coaches and child-friendly approach, we're not just teaching sports - we're building confident, healthy, and happy young athletes!

Our Programs

Multisport Fitness

🌟 Fun-Filled Fitness Adventures!

Our multisport fitness program is designed to keep kids active, engaged, and excited about movement! Through games, obstacle courses, and creative exercises, children develop fundamental motor skills, coordination, and strength while having a blast. Perfect for kids aged 4-12 who want to explore different sports and build a strong foundation for an active lifestyle!

Basketball Court Rental

🏀 Premium Basketball Experience!

Our professional-grade basketball court is available for private bookings, team practices, and birthday parties! Featuring high-quality flooring, adjustable hoops for different age groups, and excellent lighting. Perfect for serious training or just having fun with friends and family.
